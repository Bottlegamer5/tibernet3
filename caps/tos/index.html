THE LUCOZARNO PACT 2023

From now until the end of time, these clauses will apply to any bottle cap collector, otherwise known as, Bottle Cappers.

Clause 1: Conditions of Piracy (CP)

Piracy will hereby be considered legal in any competition.

Clause 2: Forevermore Objective Oath (FOO)

Any Capper that signs this document herby agrees to swear an oath of allegiance to Capping [Bottle Cap Collecting] and the other clauses in this document.

Clause 3: Warrant Of Allegiance on Homefront (WOAH)

Up to daily checkups are warranted to take place from any team towards any other. If no more than 4 bottle caps are collected in one week [Monday-Sunday], the Capper [Bottle Cap Collecter] must forfeit 2 caps from their collection. If the Capper [Bottle Cap Collecter] is unable to meet this requirement, they will be in debt towards the Capping [Bottle Cap Collecting] community and will have to pay it out. If the debt is not payed within a week one of two punishments can be used by another team (See Clause 4).


Clause 4: Punishments Of Objecting Powers(POOP)

Any Team [Power] that defies the Lucozarno Pact can be punished by other teams [Power] that are not at that time in conflict with the rules of the Lucozarno Pact.

Punishments Include:
1. Warrant for any team to search the conflicting team by force or otherwise.
2. If over 50% of teams agree, then the conflicting team can be nerfed [Evicted / Ejected] from the competition [And be banned forevermore]

Clause 5: Warrant Of Photo Evidence (WOPE)

Anyone who agrees to this pact hereby agrees to provide photo evidence at a maximum of 1 per week if any other team asks them for it.

The Bottle Cap Non-Proliferation Protocol

February 2023

This paper hereby clarifies the cap-legal standard for Bottle Cap collecting from now, until the END OF TIME ITSELF.

Bottle Caps

From here on, the cap-legal definition of a ‘Bottle Cap’ is an object that meets the following criteria:

- The item is intended to be disposable
- The item was designed to be used as a bottle cap and is designed in such a way that is plausible to be used as such.
- The item MUST be a connecting piece that connects via a knurled screw mechanism or snap-fit or other mechanical means. It must not be only a stopper as these items do not qualify as cap-legal Bottle Caps. Corks and such are STRICTLY NOT cap-legal.

Notable criteria that do qualify as cap-legal are:

- The item may be made of any material, including metal which is commonly used for beer caps.
- The item may be of any size.
- The item may be manufactured by the capper [Bottle Cap Collector] and it will still be cap-legal.

Bottle Cap Standard

From here on, cap-legal caps will be the only standard allowed in competitions of any kind tracked on websites such as https://captrack.netlify.app or similar.

Cap-Legal caps may also be referred to as Match-Caps, Capper Caps or Good Caps.
