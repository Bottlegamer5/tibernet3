THE LUCOZARNO PACT

 

From now until the end of time (or according to TOS change), these clauses will apply to any bottle cap collector, otherwise known as, Bottle Cappers, within the bounds of the Bottle Capping [Bottle Cap Collecting] Competition.

 

Clause 1: Conditions of Piracy (CP)

Piracy from Cappers will hereby be considered cap-legal in this competition.

Small-scale piracy of up to 2 cap-legal caps per day from non-Cappers is also hereby considered cap-legal for competition purposes

 

Piracy from anyone is also hereby allowed even if those caps were Capiferated, but those Capiferated caps will still count as having been Capiferated

 

Clause 2: Forevermore Objective Oath (FOO)

Any Capper that signs this document hereby agrees to swear an oath of Capping [Bottle Cap Collecting] and the other clauses in this document.

Clause 3: Punishments Of Objecting Powers (POOP)

Any Team [Power] that defies the TOS can be punished by other teams [Power] that are not at that time in conflict with the rules of the Lucozarno Pact.

This can also be invoked if another team Capiferates and tries to pass those Capiferated caps as cap-legal.

 

Punishments Include:

●       Warrant for any team to search the conflicting team by force or otherwise.

●       If a majority of teams agree, then the team that defied the Pact team can be nerfed [Evicted / Ejected] from the competition [And be banned forevermore]

●       An uncapbiased [Unbiased in relation to the capping competition] party can decide on a suitable punishment

Clause 4: Warrant Of Photo Evidence (WOPE)

Anyone who agrees to this pact hereby agrees to provide photo evidence at a maximum of 1 per day if any Capper asks them for it.

Clause 5: Cap Reporting

●        Cappers must report at least once a week if that week is active [active weeks are weeks when the capper’s [Bottle Cap Collector’s] cap count increases]

●        The reported bottle cap amount may not be more than ten caps from absolute cap-legal accuracy

●        The reported cap number must be an integer (it can be negative if the capper in question is in debt)

 

The Bottle Cap Non-Proliferation Protocol

 

February 2023

 

This paper hereby clarifies the cap-legal standard for Bottle Cap collecting from now, until the END OF TIME ITSELF.

 

  1. Bottle Caps

From here on, the cap-legal definition of a ‘Bottle Cap’ is an object that meets the following criteria:

 

●       The item is intended to be disposable

●       The item was designed to be used as a bottle cap and is designed in such a way that is plausible to be used as such.

●     The item MUST be a connecting piece that connects via a knurled screw mechanism or snap-fit or other mechanical means. It must not be only a stopper as these items do not qualify as cap-legal Bottle Caps. Corks and such are STRICTLY NOT cap-legal.

 

Notable criteria:

 

●        The item may be made of any material, including metal which is commonly used for beer caps.

●       The item may be of any size.

●       The item may not be manufactured by the capper [Bottle Cap Collector] and it will not be cap-legal.

 

  1. Bottle Cap Standard

From here on, cap-legal caps will be the only standard allowed in competitions of any kind tracked on websites such as https://captrack.netlify.app or similar.

 

Cap-Legal caps may also be referred to as Match-Caps, Kaps, Capper Caps or Good Caps.

Sufficiently Transparent Definitions

 

This clause will outline the definition of a bottle cap in further detail than the definition clause earlier in the document. This short section is meant as a rule book to account for any type of bottle cap possible.

 

Clause 1: Definition Of Removable Kaps (DORK)

Conditions for a Bottle Cap to be considered cap-legal:

●       Cannot be severely deformed as such it is unrecognizable as a bottle cap

●       Cannot be originally an attached part of the bottle, that isn’t intended to be removed

 

Extra Definitions Policy (EDP)

Clause 1: Capiferation

Important Definitions:

Capiferation

noun, manufacture, proliferation, purchase, or otherwise illicit gain other than Piracy of any amount of cap-legal caps for cap-legal competitive purposes.

 

Capiferation is STRICTLY prohibited in the Capping [Bottle Cap Collecting] Competition. Cap-Illegal caps, otherwise known as Capiferated-Caps, Bad-Caps, Crap-Caps, Brokey-Caps, or Trash-Caps are allowed to be manufactured or used by a Capper [Bottle Cap Collector] as long as it is not intended for cap-legal purposes and is not used in the competition, for example, as a commemorative item.

Requirements for Capship for any Cap-Legal Cap:

●       Must not be a foil or wrapper e.g. on most yogurt containers or similar

●       Must not be manufactured for cap-legal competition purposes

●       Must not be manufactured by a Capper or someone in contact with a Capper

●       Must not be Capiferated by anyone in the competition or in contact with anyone in the competition

●       Must not be broken that would hinder its use in its intended use case in any meaningful way

●       Must not have holes that would cause noticeable leakage or spillage

●       Must be for the purpose of suitably containing a liquid inside or preventing contamination of an environment due to the substance inside

●       May not be Capiferated in any way by Cappers or contacts of Cappers
